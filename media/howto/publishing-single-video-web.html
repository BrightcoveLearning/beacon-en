How To: Publish a Single Video to the Web

Steps

  1. Open the Media module.
  2. Click All videos in the panel navigation.
  3. Check the box next to the video to publish.
  4. Click Publish to Web.
  5. In the Select Player dropdown list, select a player to use.
  6. Click the URL below the preview player to open it in a new browser tab. Confirm that the video appears and can be played.
  7. Copy the embed code and paste it into your webpage.
