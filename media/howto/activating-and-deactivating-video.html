How To: Activate and Deactivate a Video

The Status column on the Media page indicates if a video is Activated or Deactivated.

  • - Indicates the video is active
  • - Indicates the video is inactive (can't be played)

If the Status column is not displayed, use the column selector to select it.

Steps

  1. To Activate a video, select it and then click More > Activate.
  2. To Deactivate a video, select it and then click More > Deactivate.
