Introduction

Offline Playback allows users to download video content on their mobile device and watch it later when they are not connected. Not all videos may be available for offline playback. If a download button appears for the content, then users can download Episodes and Movies to watch later.

From the Brightcove Beacon app, you can access a list of all of your downloaded content. Here, you can play and delete your downloaded videos. When you are online, you can renew an expired asset.

Features

Users

  • Content configured for Offline Playback will have a button for download
  • Download videos on iOS and Android apps for mobile devices and tablets
  • Download both DRM-protected and clear (non-encrypted) videos and watch it when not connected
    • Content will play on the device if the viewer travels to another region that is not supported by the rights settings until they reconnect on the mobile or wifi network
  • Ability to cancel, pause or delete a download
  • Ability to choose a bitrate for the download
  • When download is complete, the network can be disconnected or unavailable and videos play from the device
  • Audio and subtitles should be available whent the video is downloaded
  • Option to delete or renew an asset when it is expired
  • If the viewer's subscription becomes invalid for any reason, playback will be blocked when they reconnect to the network

Customers

  • You can set the duration of an asset availability in the Beacon CMS; it is automatically deleted from the device at the end of the window
  • Server-Side Ads (SSAI) ads are not delivered for content playing from the device

Requirements

Enable your account

Contact Brightcove customer support for the following:

  • Enable your Video Cloud Studio account for Offline Playback
  • Enable your Brightcove Beacon apps for Offline Playback
    • Apps can be configured to offer Offline Playback for all viewers or it can be a premium feature, available through a subscription

Manage your content

Make sure your content is available for Offline Playback:

  • In Video Cloud Studio, ingest videos using an ingest profile that produces a downloadable asset (see steps below)

    Ingesting content with a downloadable asset will increase customer storage cost.
  • In Video Cloud Studio, set videos to enable Offline Playback (see steps below)
  • In Beacon CMS, set which assets will be available for Offline Playback (see steps below)

Setup instructions

Follow these steps to enable video assets for Offline Playback:

You need to complete setup steps in both Video Cloud Studio and Beacon CMS.

Video Cloud Studio

These steps will be completed in Video Cloud Studio:

  1. When you upload or retranscode a video, make sure that you use an ingest profile that includes a downloadable asset (MP4).

    Ingesting content with a downloadable asset will increase customer storage cost.

  3. In the Video Information section, select Edit.

  4. Select Enable offline playback. You will do this for all downloadable assets.

  5. Click Save.

These steps will be completed in Beacon CMS:

Offline playback can be set in Beacon at several levels to support the business objectives of the content owner.

Entire Account

You can set Offline Playback for all assets in your account.

  2. In Settings, select Assets.

  3. For Allow Downloads?, select Yes.
