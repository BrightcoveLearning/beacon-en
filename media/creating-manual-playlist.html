Creating a Manual Playlist

In this topic you will learn how to create a manual playlist.

Steps

  1. Open the Media module.
  2. In the side navigation panel, click the Create Playlist link.
  3. Set the Playlist type to Manual.
  4. Enter a Playlist Name.
  5. Check Show Playlist in Sidebar to have the playlist appear in the sidebar.
  6. Click Save.
  7. To add videos to the playlist, click All Videos in the side navigation panel to display all the videos in the account.
  8. Click the checkbox next to videos you want to add to the playlist.
  9. Click More > Add To Playlist.
  10. Select a playlist and then click Add.

To rearrange the order of the videos, select the playlist and then click on a row and drag it to a new position in the list.
