Series

The Series page is used to upload and manage videos and to create series and seasons. The body of the page will list all series that have been created.

Publish / Unpublish Series

The Status column in the series list indicates if a series is published or unpublished:

  • - Series is published
  • - Series is unpublished

To publish a series:

  1. Select the checkbox next to the series name.
  2. Click .

To unpublish a series:

  1. Select the checkbox next to the series name.
  2. Click .

Series that are unpublished will not appear in Beacon apps.
