Introduction

As a Brightcove Beacon customer you now can use Engagement Insights for Beacon. This product helps you understand how subscribers use your Beacon app and provides a baseline success rate for encouraging customers to keep coming back. Engagement Insights reveals the audience's interaction with your app, including detailed video consumption trends, average days streamed, and activity over time.

Setup

Engagement Insights are available to Beacon customers for an additional charge. Contact your Brightcove OTT Delivery Team to enable your account.

Navigation

Once your account is enabled for Engagment Insights, do the following:

  1. Login to Brightcove Audience Insights.

  2. Here you will see three tabs related to Engagement Insights:

    • Summary
    • Engagement
    • Content
