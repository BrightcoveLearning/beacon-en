Archiving Pages

In this topic you will learn how to archive pages.

Archiving a page will unpublish it and remove it from the application. Pages that are archived can be deleted. To archive a page, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Pages page.
  2. In the side navigation panel, click on a page.
  3. Click Archive Page.

To view all archived pages, click the Archived Pages link at the bottom of the page.

To restore a page that has been archived, click Restore next to the page name. To permanently delete the page, click Delete.
