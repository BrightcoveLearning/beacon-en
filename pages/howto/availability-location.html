How To: Configure Location Availability

Pages can be configured so viewing is permitted or denied in specific locations. To configure the locations for a page, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Pages page.
  2. In the side navigation panel, click on a page.
  3. In the Availability section, click on Location. The location settings will display.
  4. In the Permitted Locations section, click + Location.
  5. Type the name of a country. As you begin typing the list will filter.
  6. Click on a country to select it.
  7. In the Denied Locations section, click + Location and add the countries where the page cannot be viewed.
  8. Click Save.
