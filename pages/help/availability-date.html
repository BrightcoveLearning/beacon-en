How To: Configure the Page Schedule

Pages can be configured with a schedule that defines the start date and time that a page will be available for viewing. Optionally, an end date and time can also be configured. To configure a schedule for a page, follow these steps

  1. Open the Pages page.
  2. In the side navigation panel, click on a page.
  3. In the Availability section, click on Schedule. The schedule settings will display.
  4. Click the calendar icon () and pick a start date.
  5. Click the clock icon () and pick a start time.
  6. (Optional) Check the End Time checkbox and select an end date and time.
  7. Click Save.
