Archive page

The link, located in the Panel Actions at the bottom of the Navigation Panel, will only appear if you have archived pages.

  1. In Panel Action, click .
  2. Click next to page title.
  3. In the dialog that opens, on the bottom, left side click Archive page.
  4. Note that the dialog closes and the archived page is no longer in the page list.
