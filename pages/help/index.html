Delete or restore an archived page

The Archived Pages link, located at the bottom of the page list in the Navigation Panel (called the panel actions area), appears only if you have archived pages.

  1. In Panel Actions, click Archived Pages.
  2. From the list of archived pages, click either Delete or Restore.
Change page title
  1. Click next to page name. You can change the title for other enabled language here also.
Change page order

  1. To change the order of pages, click on a page and drag it to a new position.
