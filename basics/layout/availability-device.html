How To: Configure Device Availability

Pages can be configured to appear on only certain devices. To configure the permitted/denied devices for a page, follow these steps

When you make changes to the settings a circle with an arrow inside it animates to indicate your changes are automatically saved.
  1. Open the Layout module.
  2. In the side navigation panel, hover over the page for which you wish to configure permitted/denied devices and click .
  3. In the Page Settings dialog, click Availability.
  4. In the far right of the Device restrictions row, click the toggle button.
  5. Check the boxes to allow viewing of a page from the group or individual device.
You can also change the permitted/denied devices by clicking on the page, then in Page Settings click Devices in the Availability section.
