This dialog prompts you to choose the type of page you wish to create. Your options are:

  • MAIN MENU: Pages can be in the main navigation of your app, located at the top of the app, as highlighted here.
  • SECONDARY: Pages can be used as custom View all pages, or placed in a playlist. For full details see the Managing Secondary Pages document.
