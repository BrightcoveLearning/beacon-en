How To: Configure Device Availability

Pages can be configured so they can only be viewed on specific devices. To configure the devices a page can be viewed on, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Pages page.
  2. In the side navigation panel, click on a page.
  3. In the Availability section, click on Devices. The device settings will display.
  4. Select the devices that can view the page.
  5. Click Save.
