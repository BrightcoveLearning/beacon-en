How To: Configure the Page Schedule

Pages can be configured with a schedule that defines the start date and time that a page will be available for viewing. Optionally, an end date and time can also be configured. To configure a schedule for a page, follow these steps

When you make changes to the settings a circle with an arrow inside it animates to indicate your changes are automatically saved.
  1. Open the Layout module.
  2. In the side navigation panel, hover over the page for which you wish to provide a schedule and click .
  3. In the Page Settings dialog, click Availability.
  4. In the far right of the Calendar restrictions row, click the toggle button. The schedule settings display.
  5. Click the calendar icon () and pick a start date, or manually enter a date.
  6. Click the clock icon () and pick a start time, or manually enter a time.
  7. (Optional) Check the End Time checkbox and select an end date and time.

If you schedule a published page to be available for a future date, the page status label displaying Scheduled reflects that:

You can also change the schedule by clicking on the page, then in Page Settings click Schedule in the Availability section.
