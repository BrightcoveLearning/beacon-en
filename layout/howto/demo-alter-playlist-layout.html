How To: Alter a Playlist Layout

In this topic you will learn how to alter a playlist layout.

Steps

  1. Open the Pages page.
  2. In the side navigation panel, click TV Series.
  3. In the side navigation panel, click Top Series.
  4. Choose the layout options for your TV series. For example, you can do the following:
  5. Toggle off Show video title.
  6. For Thumbnail size, select Large.
  7. Close the Top Series layout dialog.
