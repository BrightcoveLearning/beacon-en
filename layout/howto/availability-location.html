How To: Configure Location Availability

Pages can be configured to appear in only certain locations. To configure permitted/denied locations, follow these steps

When you make changes to the settings a circle with an arrow inside it animates to indicate your changes are automatically saved.
  1. Open the Layout module.
  2. In the side navigation panel, hover over the page for which you wish to configure permitted/denied locations and click .
  3. In the Page Settings dialog, click Availability.
  4. In the far right of the Location restrictions row, click the toggle button.
  5. Select the locations for both the desired permitted and denied locations by typing the name of a country/location. As you begin typing the list will filter.
You can also change the permitted/denied locations by clicking on the page, then in Page Settings click Locations in the Availability section.
