How To: Upload Videos

In this topic you will learn how to upload videos to a Brightcove Beacon account.

Steps

  1. In Beacon Studio, click Upload to open the Upload page.
  2. Click Browse for Videos and browse and select one or more files to upload or Drag and Drop files from the file system to the Upload module.
  3. The files will begin processing. Remain on the Upload page (and don't refresh the page) until all videos have a status of Processing Complete.
  4. Click View and edit video details to view the video and edit the video details in the Media page.
