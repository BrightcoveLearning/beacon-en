Introduction

section-title

  1. Log in to your OneTrust account.
  2. From the Welcome page, click the Cooke Compliance option.
  3. In the left navigation, click Templates.
  4. Choose the template you wish to copy for your template and click the ellipses icon on the far right.
  5. From the menu that appears, click Copy Template.
  6. Provide a name for your template and click Copy Template. You might have several templates, so meaningful names are important, like Web-[CustomerName]-GDPR.
