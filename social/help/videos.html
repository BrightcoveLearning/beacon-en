Videos

Publish videos from your Video Cloud account to YouTube, Facebook and Twitter.

Before you can publish videos, you must create publishing destinations for your social accounts. In the left navigation, click Destinations.
Manually publishing videos is independent from Auto Sync. If a video is tagged to be published with Auto Sync, it will do so even if you have manually published the video.
Video rendition

To distribute videos to a social platform, videos should have at least one of the following:

  • MP4 rendition
  • Video master

Videos uploaded using one of the standard ingest profiles will save a master by default.

When Brightcove Social publishes a video to a social platform:

  • Attempts to distribute the highest quality rendition that meets the rules of the social platform
  • If the rendition sent to the social platform is rejected, there is no attempt to send another rendition

Rules to rank videos:

  • MP4 assets are better than any other video container
  • The Video with the higher encoding rate is considered better
  • The video with the higher resolution is considered better
Publish to social media
  1. Select a video to publish.
  2. Click Publish.
  3. Select a social destination to publish to, or click Add New Destination.
  4. Review the video metadata properties for each destination that you are publishing to.
  5. Click Publish to Destination.

Manually publishing videos is a one-time operation, and these videos will not receive metadata updates from the Social module after the initial upload.

Set YouTube properties

By default, videos up to 15 minutes long can be uploaded to YouTube.

Video thumbnail source

  • YouTube - YouTube will select a thumbnail
  • Video Cloud - Use the Video Cloud thumbnail image as the YouTube thumbnail
  • Custom Image - Browse and select a custom thumbnail image to use

Checking Use for all destinations will use this thumbnail for all destinations currently being published to.

Updates to thumbnail images in Studio will not update the thumbnail image on the YouTube side.

Post Info properties

  • Title - Title of the video (defaults to Video Cloud title); YouTube supports up to 100 characters
  • Description - Description of the video (defaults to Video Cloud short description); YouTube supports up to 5000 characters
  • YouTube Category - Content category for the video
  • Tags - Tags for the video (defaults to Video Cloud tags); you can use max 30 characters per tag, and the text field accepts up to 500 characters

Schedule properties

  • Publish Immediately - To publish the video immediately
  • Schedule Publish Date - To choose a publish date and time for the video
The schedule date and time are sent to YouTube only once, when the video is sent to YouTube. If the schedule date and/or time needs to be changed, they must be changed in YouTube.

Captions properties

  • Use Video Cloud captions - Use captions associated with the video in Video Cloud
  • No captions - No captions will be sent to YouTube
  • Upload captions - Select a captions file, which should be in .VTT or .SRT format
Videos with closed captions clipped using Social will not have captions. You will need to upload new captions to the clipped video.

Visibility properties

  • Public - Anyone can view the video
  • Unlisted - Anyone who has the link to the video can view it
  • Private - Video can only be seen by you and the users you select

Selecting Notify subscribers when video is posted to YouTube may send a notification to your subscribers that the video was uploaded

License properties

  • Standard YouTube License
  • Creative Commons - Attribution
